# Refund Policy

## Return Window

Customers can request a full refund within 30 days of purchase, as long as the item is unused and in its original packaging. Refund requests submitted after 30 days will not be accepted under standard policy.

## Shipping Costs

Original shipping costs are non-refundable except in cases of defective or mis-shipped merchandise. Return shipping is paid by the customer unless the item arrived damaged.

## Refund Processing Time

Approved refunds are issued to the original payment method within 5 to 7 business days after we receive the returned item.
