# Warranty Terms

## Coverage Period

All products come with a one-year manufacturer warranty covering defects in materials and workmanship, starting from the date of purchase.

## What Is Covered

The warranty covers manufacturing defects, premature component failure, and faulty electronics under normal use conditions.

## What Is Not Covered

Damage caused by misuse, unauthorized modification, water exposure, or normal wear and tear is not covered under this warranty. Accessories and consumable parts are excluded.
