# Shipping Information

## Domestic Shipping

Standard domestic shipping takes 3 to 5 business days and is handled by our regional carrier partners. Expedited shipping (1 to 2 business days) is available at checkout for an additional fee.

## International Shipping

International orders typically arrive within 7 to 14 business days, depending on customs processing in the destination country. Customers are responsible for any import duties or taxes.

## Order Tracking

A tracking number is emailed automatically once an order ships, and can also be found in the order history section of your account.
